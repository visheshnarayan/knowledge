Question: Who are Mr. and Mrs. Dursley and where do they live?
Routed Topic: Literary Fiction

Based on the context provided, Mr. and Mrs. Dursley are the aunt and uncle of Harry Potter. They live at 4 Privet Drive with their son Dudley. The Dursleys are described as completely ordinary people who dislike anything out of the ordinary, including the idea of Harry's wizarding abilities.
