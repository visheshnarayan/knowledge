Question: Who are Mr. and Mrs. Dursley and where do they live?
Routed Topic: Literary Fiction
Agent Response: 

Based on the context provided, Mr. and Mrs. Dursley are Harry Potter's aunt and uncle who live on Privet Drive. Mr. Dursley is described as a big, beefy man who is the director of a company called Grunnings that makes drills. Mrs. Dursley is thin and blonde, and they have a son named Dudley. They are Muggles (non-magical people) who do not like the wizarding world that Harry comes from. Harry is brought to live with them after his parents are killed by Voldemort, as they are his only remaining relatives.
